Tracking

1. We offer a range of shipping options to suit your needs, including standard, express, and freight services. Once your shipment is dispatched, you can easily track its progress using the unique tracking number provided in your shipping confirmation email. Simply enter the tracking number on our website, and you'll get real-time updates on the current location and estimated delivery time of your package.

Shipping costs are calculated based on various factors, including the package's weight, dimensions, destination, and the selected shipping service. You can obtain an accurate shipping quote during the checkout process on our website. Additionally, please be aware that customs duties, taxes, and other fees may apply, depending on your destination country. These charges are the responsibility of the recipient and are not included in our shipping fees.

We prioritize the safety and security of your shipments throughout the entire shipping process. Our packaging standards adhere to industry best practices to prevent damage during transit. Additionally, we partner with reputable carriers and employ advanced tracking systems to monitor your shipment's journey. For high-value or sensitive shipments, we offer insurance options to provide an extra layer of protection. If you have specific concerns or requests regarding the security of your shipment, feel free to contact our customer service team for personalized assistance.
